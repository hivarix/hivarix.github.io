Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a container?

You can browse our containers page, select your preferred unit, and request a quote through our contact form.

Do you offer international delivery?

Yes. We deliver containers globally through our logistics network. Delivery times depend on your location.

Can I lease a container instead of buying?

Absolutely. We provide flexible leasing options for both short-term and long-term needs.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, bank transfers, and corporate payment agreements.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via the Contact Page or email support@hivarix.com for assistance.
