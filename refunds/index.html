Sales & Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

This Sales and Refund Policy outlines the terms governing payments and refunds for services provided by HiVaRiX.

2. Payments

All services require payment as agreed before or during project execution. Payments may be one-time or milestone-based depending on the project scope.

3. Non-Refundable Services

Due to the nature of digital and consulting services, most payments are non-refundable once work has commenced.

4. Eligibility for Refunds

  • Service was not delivered as agreed
  • Duplicate payment was made

5. Partial Refunds

Where work has begun, refunds may be issued partially based on the portion of work completed.

6. Refund Process

To request a refund, contact us with full details of your transaction. Approved refunds will be processed within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Chargebacks

Unauthorized chargebacks may result in service suspension and legal action. We encourage clients to contact us first to resolve issues.

8. Changes to Policy

HiVaRiX reserves the right to update this policy at any time.
